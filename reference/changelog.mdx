## June 2026

### Song Transposition — Pitch-Shift Audio & Chords <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Change a song's key without re-recording. Open a song's **Audio** card and click **Transpose** — pick the *from* and *to* keys and LightLoop pitch-shifts the track to match **while preserving its tempo**. When you assign audio, LightLoop also **auto-detects its key** in the background. The original file is always kept, so revert is one click. Turn on **Sync to Audio** and the stage chord chart's display key follows every transposition automatically.

See the [Transposition section](/web-app/songs#transposition).

---

### Chords on Stage — ChordPro with Smart Key Detection <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Add inline **ChordPro `[chords]`** to any song and they render above the lyrics on stage and confidence displays — the TV Wall **Stage Display** and the iOS Remote's **Stage Mode** — while the audience screen stays clean, lyrics-only. LightLoop reads the chords and **detects the key** with real music-theory scoring, using the dominant chord to tell a key from its relative minor (`Em C G D` → G, `Bm D A G…` → D). Set a separate **Display key** to transpose the whole chart on the fly; chord charts and the "Next" preview transpose too.

See the [Chords section](/web-app/songs#chords).

---

### Overlays — Watermark, QR & Weather <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

A new **Overlay** playlist item action floats a layer in a corner above your content (above everything but a countdown banner). Three types: a **Watermark** image (logo/sponsor/"live" bug, with opacity), a **QR Code** from any URL (custom colors + caption), or live **Weather** for a US ZIP code (°F, refreshed every 60s). Choose **This item** or **Until cleared** so a watermark can ride across a whole playlist until you hide it. Renders on Apple TV, TV Wall Program output, and the shared preview page.

See the [Playlists guide](/web-app/playlists#overlays).

### Playlist Attachments — Downloadable Hand-outs for Teachers <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Attach files to any playlist — lesson plans, game ideas, print-outs — from the **Share** dialog. Anyone previewing the playlist sees a **Downloads** list on the preview page and can grab the files before pressing play, no account required. Attachments work with both **Public Preview Links** and **Share with Another LightLoop Account** — when a playlist is shared to another account, its files travel with it.

See the [Sharing guide](/web-app/sharing).

### Sermon Builder — Build a Whole Teaching in One Item <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

A new **Sermon** content type lets a pastor or presenter build an entire teaching, talk, or presentation as one item — an ordered list of mixed **slides**: styled text/notes, scripture (from the Bible integration), full-screen media (image/video/audio with blur fill, trim, and stop/loop/advance), and intentional blank slides. Add a **Background media** behind text slides and a **Title media** for blanks. Build it in the drag-and-drop **Sermon Builder** (drop files between slides, ⌥↩ to split text), then advance slide-by-slide from the iOS Remote. Renders natively on Apple TV and in TV Wall mode.

See the [Sermons guide](/web-app/sermons).

### More Bible Translations — 1,200+ Across 1,000+ Languages <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

The Bible translation picker (Media Library and Sermon Builder) now offers a second source — the **Free Use Bible** library — below the YouVersion presets. A reliable fallback when a YouVersion translation is temporarily unavailable, and the way to display non-English scripture.

### TV Wall Output — Four Outputs From One Apple TV <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Drive **four independent 1080p outputs** from a single Apple TV using a low-cost 2×2 HDMI wall controller. Enable **TV Wall Output** on any feed, then assign each quadrant: **Program Output**, **Stage Display**, **Media** (independent looping image/video), or **Empty**.

Overlay quadrants render song/Bible **Lower-Thirds**, plus an **Overlay Alpha (DSK)** mode that outputs the lower-third as a solid-white fill matte on black for downstream keying in ATEM and other switchers — so one Apple TV can feed your audience screen, a confidence monitor, and a livestream key/fill at the same time.

See the [TV Wall Output guide](/apple-tv/tv-wall).

## May 2026

### LightLoop Remote — iOS Companion App <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

A free iPhone and iPad app that connects wirelessly to a nearby Apple TV running LightLoop. No account required — connection happens automatically over your local network using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework.

**Control Mode** — browse all playlists, tap any item to trigger it on the Apple TV, and monitor a live 16:9 preview of the TV output. Song items expand inline to show all lyric slides; Bible items expand verse by verse. A timeline scrubber handles video and audio seeking. On iPad, the preview floats as a picture-in-picture panel over the full grid, or expands to full width above it.

**Stage Mode** — a large-text confidence display for worship leaders and speakers. The current slide fills the top of the screen; the next slide or item is previewed below. Navigation via tap zones, swipe, or Bluetooth page-turner (arrow keys and spacebar). On landscape iPhone, the current slide goes fullscreen with a next-item strip at the bottom.

**Multi-device sessions** — connect multiple iOS devices simultaneously. A common setup: iPhone as operator controller in Control Mode, iPad as confidence display in Stage Mode. All devices stay in sync and any device can send commands.

See [LightLoop Remote](/ios-remote/overview) for full documentation.

### Songs — Synchronized Lyric Slides <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

A new media type purpose-built for worship lyrics. Create a song in the web-based Song Editor — type your slides, link an audio track and background, then record timing markers in real time or fine-tune them in the Marker Editor (an interactive waveform timeline with drag-and-drop handles, zoom levels, and ¹⁄₃₀ s arrow-key nudging). On Apple TV, lyrics cross-dissolve in sync with the music.

**Requires Apple TV app version 1.1+.**

Full style controls: font family, size, weight, italic, all-caps, color, alignment, vertical alignment, drop shadow, text stroke, and a text background color/opacity for contrast over busy visuals.

See the [Songs guide](/web-app/songs) for full documentation.
