## May 2026

### Songs — Synchronized Lyric Slides <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

A new media type purpose-built for worship lyrics. Create a song in the web-based Song Editor — type your slides, link an audio track and background, then record timing markers in real time or fine-tune them in the Marker Editor (an interactive waveform timeline with drag-and-drop handles, zoom levels, and ¹⁄₃₀ s arrow-key nudging). On Apple TV, lyrics cross-dissolve in sync with the music.

**Requires Apple TV app version 1.1+.**

Full style controls: font family, size, weight, italic, all-caps, color, alignment, vertical alignment, drop shadow, text stroke, and a text background color/opacity for contrast over busy visuals.

See the [Songs guide](/web-app/songs) for full documentation.

---

### Marker Editor <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

An interactive waveform timeline for fine-tuning lyric timing markers on Song items. Drag marker handles directly on the audio waveform, zoom from overview to sample-level detail, select a marker and nudge with arrow keys at 33 ms steps, and watch a live slide preview with cross-dissolve transitions as you scrub.

---

### Text Background Color for Lyrics <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Song style settings now include a **Text Background** color and opacity control. Adds a colored panel behind lyric text — useful for ensuring readability over high-contrast or busy background visuals.

## April 2026

### Planning Center Check-In Monitor <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(232,168,76,0.12)',color:'#E8A84C',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>Integration</span>

A new display type that shows a live roster of who's checked in via Planning Center — updated in real time. Great for hallway TVs and volunteer stations.

### PCO Calendar Display <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(232,168,76,0.12)',color:'#E8A84C',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>Integration</span>

Lobby and hallway TVs can now display today's schedule pulled live from Planning Center — service times, events, and room assignments. Automatically refreshes throughout the day.

### Item-Level Action Soundtracks <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Individual playlist items can now trigger a specific soundtrack when they appear on screen — not just the playlist-wide soundtrack.

## March 2026

### Background Soundtracks <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Assign a music playlist to any content playlist. LightLoop plays it on loop in the background while the Apple TV displays your slides and videos. Controls for volume, shuffle, and loop are in the playlist settings panel.

---

### Scheduled Playlists <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Set a countdown on any playlist — when time expires, LightLoop automatically transitions to the next playlist.

---

## February 2026

### Welcome Party — Planning Center <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

LightLoop's Welcome Party feature now works with Planning Center Check-Ins. When a child checks in via PCO, a full-screen celebration with their name appears on every Apple TV in the room — automatically, within about a second of check-in.

---

### Offline Media Caching <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(76,175,121,0.15)',color:'#4CAF79',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>Improved</span>

The Apple TV app now pre-caches all playlist media in the background so screens keep playing even if your internet connection drops.

---

### Google Drive Sync <span style={{fontSize:'0.7em',fontWeight:600,background:'rgba(108,141,255,0.15)',color:'#818CF8',padding:'2px 8px',borderRadius:'100px',marginLeft:'8px',verticalAlign:'middle'}}>New</span>

Connect a Google Drive folder to LightLoop and its contents become a live media source. Add or remove files from the folder and LightLoop picks up the changes automatically.
