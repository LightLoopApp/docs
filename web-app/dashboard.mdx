The LightLoop dashboard is your control center. Everything — uploading media, building playlists, configuring feeds, inviting teammates — happens here.

## Navigation

The sidebar has four main sections:

| Section | What's here |
|---------|-------------|
| **Media** | Your full library of uploaded files |
| **Playlists** | All playlists and playlist groups |
| **Feeds** | Feeds and their connected Apple TVs |
| **Account** | Profile, integrations, team members, and settings |

## Media tab

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Media tab showing a grid of thumbnails with type badges (video, image, audio, etc.) */}

The Media tab shows every file in your library. You can:

- **Upload** new files by clicking **Upload** or dragging files onto the page
- **Search** by name or filter by type (video, image, audio, web, monitor, calendar)
- **Tag** items with custom labels for easier organization
- **Select multiple items** using the checkbox on each card for bulk actions (delete, move, tag)
- **Click any item** to open its detail panel and edit metadata, add pause points, set in/out markers, or view where it's used

## Playlists tab

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Playlists tab showing grouped playlists with collapse/expand arrows on group headers */}

The Playlists tab lists all your playlists, optionally grouped into folders. You can:

- **Create** a new playlist or group
- **Drag items** between groups to reorganize
- **Click a playlist** to open the playlist editor
- **Share** a read-only preview link for stakeholder review

## Feeds tab

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Feeds tab showing two feed cards, each with their Feed ID prominently displayed */}

The Feeds tab shows every feed in your account. From here you can:

- **Create** a new feed
- **See the Feed ID** for each feed (used by Apple TVs to connect)
- **Click a feed** to open the feed editor and manage its playlists, schedule, and settings

## Account menu

Click your profile picture in the top-right corner to access:

- **Team members** — invite and manage collaborators
- **Integrations** — connect Canva, Planning Center, Google Calendar
- **Settings** — choose your Drive folder, timezone, and notification preferences
- **Sign out** / **Delete account**

## Quick actions

Anywhere in the dashboard, you can use the **+** button in the top-right corner to quickly:

- Upload media
- Create a playlist
- Create a feed
