If your team creates slides, announcements, or graphics in Canva, you can import them directly into LightLoop without downloading and re-uploading.

## Connecting your Canva account

1. Click your profile picture → **Integrations**
2. Find the **Canva** section and click **Connect Canva**
3. Authorize LightLoop to access your designs (read-only)

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Integrations page showing the Canva section with a "Connect Canva" button and the Canva logo */}

## Importing a design

Once connected, you can import designs in two ways:

### Option A: Paste a link

1. Open your design in Canva and copy its share link
2. In LightLoop's Media library, click **Import → Canva Link**
3. Paste the link and click **Import**
4. Choose the import format: **Image** or **Video**

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Canva import dialog with a pasted Canva link and the Image/Video format selector */}

### Option B: Browse your designs

1. In LightLoop's Media library, click **Import → Browse Canva**
2. Browse your Canva workspace — designs appear in a grid
3. Select a design and click **Import**
4. Choose the import format: **Image** or **Video**

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Canva design browser showing a grid of Canva designs with thumbnails */}

## Import formats

| Format | Best for |
|--------|----------|
| **Image** | Static slides, announcement graphics, title cards |
| **Video** | Animated designs, motion graphics, countdown loops |

<Tip>
  Canva's video export includes animations and transitions. If your design has animation, import as Video.
</Tip>

## Re-syncing a design

If you update a design in Canva after importing it, you can re-sync to pull in the latest version:

1. Find the item in your Media library (Canva items have a Canva badge)
2. Open the detail panel
3. Click **Re-sync from Canva**

{/* SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Canva media item detail panel showing the "Re-sync from Canva" button */}

The item is updated in place — it stays in all playlists it was already added to, and the Apple TV re-downloads the updated file automatically.

## Disconnecting Canva

1. Click your profile picture → **Integrations**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to Canva

Disconnecting removes your authorization token. Previously imported Canva items remain in your library — they're stored in Google Drive like any other file.
